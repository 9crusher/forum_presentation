AI Milestones

Key moments in the history of artificial intelligence

1

ELIZA

1966 — First AI Chatbot

Created by Joseph Weizenbaum at MIT, ELIZA simulated a Rogerian psychotherapist and became the first program to attempt natural language conversation with humans.

Wikipedia Try ELIZA Online Joseph Weizenbaum The ELIZA Effect
2

Deep Blue

1997 — IBM's Chess Champion

IBM's Deep Blue became the first computer to defeat a reigning world chess champion, Garry Kasparov, in a six-game match under standard tournament conditions.

Wikipedia Deep Blue vs Kasparov IBM History Garry Kasparov
3

Nano Banana

2024 — Nobel Prize in Chemistry

The 2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry was awarded for computational protein design and AI-based protein structure prediction. The "nano banana" is a famous example of a custom-designed protein created by David Baker's lab using AI tools.

Nobel Prize 2024 David Baker AlphaFold NobelPrize.org
4

ChatGPT

2022 — The AI Moment

OpenAI's ChatGPT brought large language models to the mainstream, reaching 100 million users in two months and sparking a global conversation about the future of AI.
